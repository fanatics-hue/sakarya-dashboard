Project Timeline
Development Milestones
August 2020
Discovery
Sakarya Gas Field discovered in the western Black Sea by TPAO using the Fatih drillship. Tuna-1 well drilled at 2,115m water depth. Reserves estimated at 540 bcm, later revised to 710 bcm (2023).
Completed
2020 → April 2023
Phase 1 — First Gas
Fast-track delivery in under 3 years. 10 subsea wells drilled, onshore Filyos processing plant built. First gas reached Filyos on 20 April 2023. Capacity: ~10 million m³/day. Contractors: SLB, Subsea Integration Alliance, Saipem, Wood.
Completed
2023 → 2027
Phase 2 — Expansion
EPCI contract awarded May 2023 to SLB + Subsea7 + Saipem consortium. 26 additional wells, 158km subsea pipeline, new Floating Production Unit. Construction started Dec 2024. Castorone pipelay vessel mobilised. First gas expected 2027. Total capacity: ~14 bcm/year.
In Progress
2025 → 2028
Phase 3 — Major Offshore Expansion
Contracts awarded Sep 2025. Saipem EPCI: 8 rigid flowlines + 183km export pipeline connecting Sakarya & Amasra fields to Filyos. Baker Hughes: deepwater trees & control systems (delivery from late 2025). Pipelay campaign: 2027. Water depth: 2,000–2,200m. Target: 30% of Turkey's gas demand.
